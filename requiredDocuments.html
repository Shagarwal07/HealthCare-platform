Required Documents

Documents Needed for Update

Name Change

Required Document: A government-issued ID showing the updated name.

Instructions: Please upload a scanned copy of the updated name proof.

Proof of New Address

Required Document: Utility bill or any government document showing the new address.

Instructions: Upload a scanned copy of the document.

Phone Number Verification

Required Document: A screenshot of your phone number verification code.

Instructions: Enter the code sent to your phone in the provided form.

Email Address Update

Required Document: A confirmation email from your updated email address.

Instructions: Verify your email address by clicking the confirmation link.
